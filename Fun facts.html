Fun Facts

  • The name Hamburger comes from the German city Hamburg
  • The book "The Art of Cookery made Plain and Easy" in 1758 by Hannah Glasse showed a recipe called the "Hamburgh Sausage" sharing similarities to the modern day Hamburger.
  • During WW1, Hamburgers were refered to as salisbury steak due to anti-German sentiment
